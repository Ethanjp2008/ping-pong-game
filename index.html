Instructions

Here you will play a ping pong game with the opponant as the computer.It is a normal ping pong game exept to move the paddle you have to move your right hand.

To move the paddle up move your right wrist up.

To move the paddle down move your right wrist down.



How To Get Points:

If you are able to return the ball you will get one point.

If you lose the ball you will lose one point
